Every Petal Tells a Story.

we do not merely sell flowers——we compose emotions, we arrange memories, we bloom poetry.

"Petal & Poem is not a shop; it is a story waiting to unfold.
Each bouquet, a stanza; each bloom, a line of poetry,
woven with the delicate threads of nature's finest silk.
Here, roses do not simply bloom—they sigh with the weight of unsent letters.
Lilies do not merely stand—they hum soft elegies for fleeting moments.
Peonies unfurl like sonnets, their fragrance lingering like a lover's touch.
For those who long to confess, we bind petals into love letters.
For those who yearn to remember, we weave flowers into memories.
For those who seek beauty in the ephemeral, we craft eternity in a single bloom.
Step into our sanctuary, where every arrangement is a verse,
every fragrance a melody, every petal a promise.
Let us write your story in flowers,
for some emotions are too tender for words— and so, they blossom.
Petal & Poem — where flowers speak the language of poetry."

About us

At Petal & Poem, we specialize in crafting elegant floral arrangements that blend timeless beauty with modern design. Our curated selection includes a diverse range of blooms—from classic roses to rare seasonal flowers—each carefully chosen to convey emotion and meaning. With a commitment to artistry and personalization, we design every bouquet to reflect the unique stories and sentiments of our customers. More than just a florist, we strive to understand your needs and transform them into floral expressions that speak directly from the heart.
